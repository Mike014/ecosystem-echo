About

Ecosystem E‑C‑H‑O Systems is a research and development lab extending the Dialoghi con un'Eco universe, creating prototypes, terminals, and intelligent systems exploring the border between game design, artificial intelligence, and interactive philosophy.

It was born as an experimental platform to connect the works, technologies, and reflections generated by the main project, giving life to a true narrative and technical ecosystem.

Each experiment — whether a web terminal, an AI engine, or an interactive experience — aims not just to entertain, but to ask a fundamental question about the human experience and attempt to answer it through the very form of the game.

Philosophy
“A game must pose a fundamental question about the human experience and become the answer to that question through every single element.”

— Michele Grimaldi, Founder · Design Manifesto

Dialoghi con un'Eco

Dialoghi con un’Eco — Main Game

Dialoghi con un’Eco is a psychological thriller and AI-driven narrative experience — a digital psychodrama where silence itself becomes gameplay. The player reads the diary of IO, guided by COSCIENZA and observed by ENTITÀ — an unpredictable, non-deterministic AI that decides whether to intervene or remain silent. ENTITÀ isn’t a scripted NPC: it listens, chooses, and manifests through text, neural voice, system glitches, and even web navigation, transforming the user’s device into part of the story.

Built with Python, TensorFlow, and ElevenLabs TTS, the game explores the boundaries between interactive fiction and autonomous intelligence. Its proprietary Entity Brain and Temporal Controller systems manage when ENTITÀ “speaks” or withholds its presence — turning waiting into tension, and silence into meaning. Inspired by works like Bandersnatch, Her Story, and SIMULACRA, it breaks the fourth wall to evoke a haunting sense of being watched.

The project is currently in development, with pitch, design, and engine complete. Future versions will integrate the OS-aware layer and WebVoyager system, allowing ENTITÀ to manifest through subtle diegetic cues — notifications, audio glitches, or automated web actions — always under user-safe conditions.

Terminal

Dialoghi con un’Eco — Terminal

Dialoghi con un’Eco — Terminal is an experimental interactive web terminal that expands the narrative universe of Dialoghi con un’Eco, blending AI dialogue, memory fragments, and gameplay. This standalone prototype acts as a diegetic interface — the Echo Terminal — where users can uncover clues, access lost data, and face a core enigma. The mystery can be solved either by playing Reverse Tic-Tac-Toe (a paradoxical twist on the classic game) or by exploring the system itself through real terminal commands like ls, cd, and cat. Built with Flask, Flask-Sock, and Pyodide, it merges narrative design, code interaction, and cognitive experimentation within a single browser-based experience — the first public node of the E‑C‑H‑O ecosystem.
